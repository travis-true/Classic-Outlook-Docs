# Classic Outlook task map source

Status: Production draft

## High-level overview

Classic Outlook task map helps employees choose the right Outlook tool in 30 to 60 seconds. It supports Sales Account Representatives, Sales Account Associates, field iPad users who also use company Windows workstations, and employees with mixed Outlook skill levels.

Core workflow: **Email → Decide → Make visible → Follow through**

Required phrases:

- Visible beats remembered.
- Copilot starts the draft. You finish the message.

## Architectural diagram concepts

Use one editable Word page with a header, a horizontal workflow banner, and six accessible table-based panels in a two-column visual map:

1. Decide what the email needs
2. Respond
3. Make follow-up visible
4. Organize and find
5. Work with shared mailboxes
6. Use Copilot

## Step-by-step setup

1. Open the generated DOCX.
2. Confirm the official BCBSKS primary logo appears at the top at least 2 inches wide.
3. Confirm the support placeholders are still visible and ready for approved links.
4. Review every procedure against the deployed Microsoft 365 tenant before publication.
5. Replace placeholders only with approved internal paths.

## Common Gotchas

- Do not move unresolved work out of sight unless the next action is visible somewhere else.
- Do not rely on Copilot output without review.
- Do not use shared mailbox sending until ownership and the From address are clear.
- Search terms and Copilot labels must be retested against the deployed Outlook build.
