# Copilot troubleshooting card design spec

Status: Production draft

## Outputs
- Letter: `Take-Control-of-Your-Inbox_Classic-Outlook_Copilot-Troubleshooting-Card_Letter_v1.0.docx`
- Desk card: `Take-Control-of-Your-Inbox_Classic-Outlook_Copilot-Troubleshooting-Card_Desk-Card_v1.0.docx`

## Format
- Letter version: US Letter portrait, one page target.
- Desk-card version: 5 × 7 inches, portrait, two pages representing front and back.
- Editable Word documents created with python-docx.
- Real headings, tables, numbered lists, and bulleted lists.

## Brand
Use BCBSKS Blue `#005EB8`, Midnight `#002855`, Bluejay `#41B6E6`, approved grays, and white. Use Warning red only for warnings. Use the official logo without cropping, stretching, recoloring, or effects. Use the full logo at least 2 inches wide where it fits.

## Accessibility
- Minimum 11-point body text where practical.
- Minimum 10-point table text.
- Descriptive headings.
- Descriptive links and placeholders.
- No essential content in floating objects.
- Do not rely on color alone.
- Include meaningful alt text for the synthetic mockup.
- Human review required before publication.
