# Copilot in Classic Outlook: Quick fixes

Status: Production draft

## High-level overview
Use this card when Copilot is missing, cannot summarize a thread, cannot draft, or gives coaching that does not fit. The card is for employees using company work accounts with Microsoft 365 Copilot enabled by BCBSKS.

Copilot starts the draft. You finish the message.

## Architectural diagram concepts
- Work account and assigned Copilot license enable access.
- Supported Classic Outlook connects to the employee's primary Exchange Online mailbox.
- Outlook connected experiences and tenant settings allow Copilot commands to appear.
- The employee opens a supported primary-mailbox message or draft.
- Copilot may summarize, draft, or coach. The employee verifies all output before sending.
- Shared, delegate, group, archive, unsupported, on-premises, or protected mailboxes require support review or a different approved path.

## Step-by-step setup
1. Sign in to Classic Outlook with your company work account.
2. Confirm you are in your primary mailbox.
3. Use an updated supported Classic Outlook version.
4. Confirm Outlook is online.
5. Use a standard, unencrypted email or draft for practice.
6. If Copilot is missing or fails, restart Outlook and test another supported message.
7. If the issue continues, use [[APPROVED COPILOT SUPPORT PATH REQUIRED]].

## Start here
Copilot in Classic Outlook may require:

- Company work account.
- Microsoft 365 Copilot license or an approved Copilot feature entitlement.
- Supported Classic Outlook version.
- Primary Exchange Online mailbox.
- Required connected experiences and tenant settings.
- Supported message type.

Do not assume Copilot is available in every mailbox or every message.

## Copilot button is missing

### Check
1. Are you using your company work account?
2. Is the message or draft in your primary mailbox?
3. Are you in Classic Outlook for Windows?
4. Is Outlook updated?
5. Did you restart Outlook?
6. Are connected experiences allowed by company settings?
7. Does Copilot appear in another supported message?

### Try
- Close and reopen Outlook.
- Open a new email in the primary mailbox.
- Open a standard unencrypted email thread.
- Check for approved Microsoft 365 updates.
- Test Copilot in another approved Microsoft 365 app.

### Get help when
- Copilot is missing from every supported Outlook message.
- Your license is active but the command never appears.
- Copilot stopped appearing after an update.

## Copilot cannot summarize the thread

### Check
- The thread is in the primary mailbox.
- It contains enough messages to summarize.
- The email is not in a shared, delegate, group, or archive mailbox.
- The message does not use unsupported encryption.
- Outlook is online.

### Try
1. Close and reopen the thread.
2. Restart Outlook.
3. Try a different low-risk thread.
4. Compare behavior in New Outlook or Outlook on the web when permitted.

## Draft with Copilot does not work

### Check
- Your prompt is clear.
- The draft is in a supported mailbox.
- Outlook is connected.
- The prompt does not ask for unsupported or missing facts.

### Improve the prompt
Use this:

> Draft a warm professional reply to this thread. Ask for the missing effective date. Keep it under 100 words. Do not add new facts.

Avoid this:

> Reply to this.

## The draft contains incorrect information
1. Do not send it.
2. Compare it with the original email.
3. Remove invented or unsupported details.
4. Correct names, dates, owners, and deadlines.
5. Add information only you know.
6. Complete the final review.

## Coaching suggestions do not fit
- Apply only suggestions that help.
- Keep required deadlines and direct requests.
- Restore context that was removed.
- Do not accept tone changes automatically.

## Final-review checklist
- Accuracy.
- Recipients.
- From address.
- Owners.
- Dates and deadlines.
- Tone.
- Missing context.
- Attachments and links.
- Sensitive details.
- Final wording.

## Common Gotchas
- Copilot output can sound confident and still be incomplete.
- Copilot in Outlook may not work in shared, delegate, group, archive, unsupported, or protected mailboxes.
- Availability can depend on license, tenant settings, app version, mailbox location, and message type.
- Coaching suggestions are suggestions, not instructions.
- Do not use Copilot output as a replacement for required approvals.
