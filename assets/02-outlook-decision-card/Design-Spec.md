# Design spec — Which Outlook am I using?

Status: Production draft

## Formats

- Half-Letter: 5.5 × 8.5 inches, portrait.
- Small card: 5 × 7 inches, portrait.

## Brand

Use BCBSKS Blue `#005EB8`, Midnight `#002855`, Bluejay `#41B6E6`, approved grays and white. Warning red is not used. Body text uses Arial at 11 points or larger. Headings use Arial Bold or Georgia Bold.

## Logo

Use `OFFICIAL BCBSKS PRIMARY LOGO.png` at 2 inches wide on the half-Letter version. Omit the logo from the 5 × 7 version because the required 2-inch primary logo would reduce decision-path readability in the smaller format. The disclosure remains on both versions.

## Accessibility

- Use real headings, lists and tables in Word.
- Do not rely on arrows alone; every branch includes Yes or No text.
- Use meaningful alt text for every thumbnail.
- Use inline images only.
- Keep all decision text selectable.
- Mark screenshots as synthetic placeholders and not version evidence until approved captures replace them.

## Screenshot and mockup approach

Approved screenshots were not found in the repository for this asset. The build creates four structured screenshot placeholders and four sanitized synthetic training mockups as editable SVG and exported PNG.
